PORTAL HISTORY

e-INEC Nigeria is the official Nigeria online portal for Nigerian election processes operated by the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC). INEC in its responsibility of ensuring more credible Nigerians are duly involved in the nation's electioneering processes have decided to establish a transparent, efficient and user-friendly online presence recognized by the provisions of the Nigerian constitution using the geometric fingerprint Identification system as its core. e-INEC Nigeria does not run any private websites or blogs and has no intention of running any. All information contained or obtained herein are statutorily censored by INEC and its open for election monitoring groups scrutiny. However, all personal details entered by the users are solely the properties of the federal republic of Nigeria and are treated as strictly confidential. Only the user is permitted access to such information for possible editing where applicable.

It should suffice to point out that the site uses the latest internet safety sophistications to ensure that all information transmitted through are safe and uncompromised. Following global best election practices, we on oath assure all users of credible free and fair elections. The entire electioneering processes shall be visibly transparent on this site.

SITE TOUR

The site is broken down into the Navigation pane, the content section, sidebars and the footer section. The most important links are briefly explained in bullets below:

Menu

Only present in smaller screens (mobiles). Contains all other links as listed below.

Home

The default link showing the general overview of the site; first page seen when site loads.

Register

Allows first time users (i.e party nominees and voters) to enter their information, create a profile and be eligible to vote and be voted for. The information provided is subject to strict scrutiny and investigation by INEC.

Login

Allows all eligible users to access their profile and edit where permissible.

Vote

The main link for the voting processes. All election links are provided here. Users are also allowed the choice of voting by sorting through parties, candidates or constituencies.

Results

Allows all site users to view the results of all ongoing elections and previously conducted elections.

Contact Us

Allows all site users the opportunity to reach INEC and its subsidiaries for any complaint or observation whatsoever.

Search

Allows for a quick site survey for specific content within the site.

ELIGIBILITY

INEC and the federal Republic of Nigeria recognizes and emphasizes universal suffrage; the right to vote and be voted for. However; for ensuring a smooth election process that abides by universally acceptable global standards, the following rules apply;

♦ All site users wishing to participate actively in the election process must be dully certified Nigerians either by birth or naturalization. No persons seeking or staying on terms of asylum are eligible to vote.

♦ Age restrictions are 18 for voters, 25 for state house of assembly, 25 for house of representatives, 30 for the senate, 30 for gubernatorial and 35 for presidency. This is in line with Nigerian constitution sections 65, 106, 131 and 177 as at amended.

♦ All voters must possess the following identification documents, valid and current where applicable: passport photograph (12*12 inches), birth certificate and a National Identification Number (NIN).

♦ The above applies to all candidates vying for public portfolios. Valid party nomination and declaration documents must also be provisible.

♦ All registrations must be at INEC accredited offices. Fingerprints must be bold and similar. Illegible fingerprints may result to inability to vote.
